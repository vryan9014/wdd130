Overview

Purpose

We believe that our job is to ensure that you enjoy rafting to the fullest; therefore, we work with hotels, and service providers to meet your expectations. Our goal is to provide an exclusive service to meet our customer's needs. We want you to take advantage of your stay and time; we care about your safety. Behind each of the services of our tours, there is a prepared, and trained human team who makes and wants your experiences to be unique. We want you never to forget your trip and that you choose us again!

Audience

You'd be hard-pressed to find people who would say they didn't get good value for the money they spent with us. For this reason, by serving families groups and experienced rafters, we provide a service with professionalism, travel organization, guides and operations personnel. We currently have equipment for more than a hundred people in the activity of rafting, fifty equipment for canopy and rappelling, and about twenty-five mountain bikes and kayaks. If you need to carry out a personal procedure or make a reservation, you can go to our offices located in Rosario. You can also contact us through our phone number 54 261 4238409 or by electronic messages to info@rufir.com.ar.

Audience

Our target audience includes families, groups, and experienced rafters who are looking for an adrenaline-pumping and unforgettable outdoor adventure. We cater to a wide range of experience levels, from those who are new to rafting to seasoned adventurers.

Scenario Questions

Scenario 1: You are a family of four who is looking for a fun and exciting activity to do together. What makes our rafting site a good fit for you?

Answer: Our rafting site offers a variety of trips that are suitable for all ages, from gentle float trips to more challenging rapids. We also have a qualified staff of guides who will ensure that your family has a safe and enjoyable experience.

Scenario 2: You are a group of friends who are looking for a weekend getaway. What makes our rafting site a good option for you?

Answer: Our rafting site is located in a beautiful natural setting and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and rivers. We also have a variety of activities available, including hiking, camping, and swimming.
